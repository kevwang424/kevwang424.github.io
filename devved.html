DevveD

A Software-as-a-Service Rails app that developers of all levels can join and get connected with other developers. With a paid subscription users are able to see contact information (email and phone number) of other users to get in touch with. There are two tiers of service, basic and pro.

Basic service does not require a credit card for sign up. Users have access to the community but are unable to see contact details of other users.

Pro service requires a credit card upon sign up. Users have access to the community and are able to see contact details of other users.
